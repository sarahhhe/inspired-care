About us

The aim of Inspired Care is to provide individualised person-centred care, primarily for individuals with disabilities, living with dementia or alzheimer’s, palliative care, or older adults with additional support needs.

Our vision

In Leicester and Leicestershire, we strive to provide holistic outstanding care that meets the physical and social needs of our valued service users.

Our mission

As an organisation, Inspired Care, provides a wide range of high-quality care to service users and their families who need support and care in their own environment, empowering them to live a fulfilling, independent and happy life.

Quality Care

Quality care encompasses a wide range of aspects related to service users' wellbeing. Providing quality care involves ensuring that the care is safe, caring, effective, patient-centred, timely, and equitable. Inspired Care implements delivery and quality improvement systems and continuously strive to improve the quality of care they provide.

Standards of Care

Different approaches have been adopted to ensure standards of quality of care are met. These include evidence-based decisions, performance evaluation, and explicit efforts to improve quality, reduce errors, and involve service users in the decision-making process.

Professionalism

This will be accomplished by our professional team with passion, dedication, and integrity. We are committed to continuously developing and improving the services we provide. Planning and implementing a clear service and support strategy.

About us

At Inspired Care, we help older and disabled adults and their families navigate the overwhelming choices and transitions that come with aging or chronic health issues.

Our services

As part of our organisation's support services, we will assist service users with tasks they are unable to perform independently or safely due to illness or incapacity. We provide care in the following regulated activities, as defined in the Health and Social Care Act 2008.

Listed below are the age groups and specialty service user bands for which Inspired Care will provide services, as defined by the Care Quality Commission:

  • Adults 18-65
  • Adults 65+
Request a callback

Personal care

Person-centered care helping individuals to maintain their independence with assistance such as bathing, toileting, dressing and eating as well as helping them to get around.

End of life / Palliative care

We provide specialist care for both end of life and palliative clients who want to return or remain in their own homes. It is an emotional time for all involved and we’re here to support through our bespoke care at home services.

Housekeeping

Our carers can help around your home with chores such as light cleaning, pet care, doing the laundry, shopping and running errands.

Companionship

Our caregivers will provide quality companionship while assisting with daily activities to meet your specific needs, including social, physical and/or wellbeing activities.

Medication management

Our carers can assist with medical support and administering medication. They can also assist with collecting prescriptions and arranging appointments on their behalf.

People living with dementia

A person living with dementia may need more care and support as their condition progresses. We take a personal and sensitive approach to dementia care as we understand that the symptoms vary quite considerably from person to person.

Individuals with disabilities

If you or a loved one is living with developmental or other disabilities, we can help. We can provide personal care, supportive care, and respectful, safe supervision—or whatever assistance is needed to live your best life in your own home.

Respite/ post-operative care

Respite stays can be tailored to each person’s individual care needs which can be from a few days or for as long as is needed. Including convalescent care after time in hospital, an operation or a period of illness.

About us

At Inspired Care, we help older and disabled adults and their families navigate the overwhelming choices and transitions that come with aging or chronic health issues.
